Robotics

Our idea for our robot design is for it to be small and good at doing missions. So we came up with different robot designs and at the end morphed it all together to make the perfect robot fit for the missions that we are going to do. We have many cool features in not just our robot, but also in our code. For our code, we have a “dashboard” to be able to access our code quickly and efficiently. Our robot has a small but mobile robot that has two color sensors that can sense two different colors. We also have a gyro sensor to be able to make accurate turns. Lastly, we have a “hub”, a hub is the place at the front of the robot that you can easily attach attachments or remove them. Overall we have a perfect robot fit for our missions that we all took time and effort to make and perfect.
